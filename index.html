About Me

Hi, I’m Ashlee, a Registered Somatic Movement Educator passionate about supporting women in reconnecting with their bodies, releasing stored tension, and cultivating deep awareness. Rooted Movement Somatics was born from a desire to create a grounded space for healing, nervous system regulation, and coming home to yourself.

Through guided somatic movement practices, I help women shift from overwhelm to restoration, weaving together holistic body–mind–spirit practices in both individual and group sessions.
